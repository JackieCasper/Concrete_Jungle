Single In the City Mixer

09/15/1991

Singles ready to mingle

Index finger tired from swiping left? Join over 150 New York singles in the 20s, 30s and 40s at this fun and flirty mixer filled with drink specials, ice breakers, music and of course, hotties. Head to Midtown’s St. Pat’s relaxed environment, where you’ll mingle with ten men or women and write the names of any who catch your eye. If you have any matches by the end of the night, you’ll be put in touch and love will soon fill the air (we hope). Come give love another chance at this exciting mixer.

22 W 46th Street, Manhattan, 10036 47th-50th Sts - Rockefeller Ctr
